What happens next?

  1. Your issue has been submitted to the Ward Member
  2. The Ward Member will review your issue
  3. If valid, it will be forwarded to both the Sarpanch and Uppasarpanch
  4. If not valid, you will be notified of the reason
  5. The Sarpanch and Uppasarpanch will review and resolve the issue
  6. You'll receive updates as your issue progresses through each authority
  7. You can track the status of your issue any time using the reference ID
