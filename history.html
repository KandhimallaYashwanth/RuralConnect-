Village History

Learn about the rich heritage and historical events of our village.

1950

Village Foundation

Our village was established in the post-independence era when several families from neighboring regions settled here.

1975

First School Established

The primary school was built to provide education to village children, marking a significant milestone in our development.

1985

Electrification

The village received electricity for the first time, transforming daily life and enabling progress.

2000

Water Supply Project

A major water supply project was completed, providing clean drinking water to all households.

2018

Digital Revolution

The village embraced technology with the establishment of a computer center and internet connectivity.

2025

RuralConnect Launch

Our village adopted the RuralConnect platform to strengthen governance and improve community engagement.

RuralConnect

Connecting rural communities with digital tools for better governance, transparency, and citizen participation.
